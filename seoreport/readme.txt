I. DESCRIPTION
--------------
This script is intended to lookup for SEO results for your domain on all major
search engines and other important sites and to present them on a single page.
It retrieves and displays the following information about your domain: google
indexed pages, google inbound links, alexa rank, alexa inbound links, dmoz
listing (if any), siteadvisor & myWOT ratings, domain age.
Note: Please do NOT ask us for Google PR inclusion, it is against Google TOS!
Simply install Google toolbar and get your site stats.


II. USAGE
---------
1. Upload seoreport.php file on your server and open in your browser:
   http://www.yourdomain.com/seoreport.php
2. View results for your site online.
